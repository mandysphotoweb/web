I am Armando Martinez. And I am delighted to have you here browsing my website. I am a photographer of social events, Weddings, Sweet sixteen, I also specialize in model photography. Etc.

Currently as a professional photographer, I love to capture your special moments such as your wedding day, christening, communion, sweet sixteen or any other social event.

In life you have to have hobbies, in addition to passions. I have been fortunate to make my work my hobby and my passion. Through my camera I live stories. Each one different, with different protagonists, true stories and unforgettable moments.

But I also discover incredible places enjoying photography on my trips. Because as I have already mentioned photography for me is not only work. I also enjoy it.

Thank you, I hope my work is to your liking.
